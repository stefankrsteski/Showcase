Pianixi - Learn Music Notes

pianixi.com

Master the art of reading piano notes with an app designed to make learning fast, simple, and fun. Whether you're a complete beginner or brushing up on your skills, Pianixi helps you recognize notes on the staff and match them to the correct keys on a piano. Train with different clefs, explore new game modes, and customize your experience with unlockable themes. The more you play, the more accurate and confident you become. Perfect for anyone learning piano or wanting to improve note recognition without the pressure of traditional lessons
