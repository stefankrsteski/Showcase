Pianixi - Learn Music Notes

pianixi.com

Pianixi helps you master reading piano notes in a fun, fast, and interactive way. Whether you're a complete beginner or brushing up on your skills, Pianixi guides you to recognize notes on the staff and match them to the correct piano keys with confidence.

Train with different clefs, explore new game modes, and customize your experience with unlockable themes. The more you play, the more accurate, confident, and fluent you become. Pianixi is perfect for anyone learning piano or improving note recognition without the pressure of traditional lessons.

• Learn Note by Note: Sharpen your skills by matching notes on the piano.
• Note Sequence Mode: Identify sequences of notes played one after another.
• Perfect Pitch: Listen and pick the right note to develop perfect pitch.
• Compose Playground: Experiment and create in your personal piano sandbox.
• All Clefs Covered: Practice Treble, Bass, Alto, and Tenor clefs for complete training.

With Pianixi, each practice session helps you build confidence, speed, and musical intuition while making learning the music notes enjoyable and interactive.
